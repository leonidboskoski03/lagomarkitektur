L-28 Stoneframe villa is a contemporary courtyard home that blends architectural clarity with everyday comfort. Clean horizontal volumes are framed by natural stone, plaster, and warm wood to create a timeless, welcoming identity. Large glazed openings connect interior spaces with private outdoor lounges, allowing light and landscape to shape the living experience.

Thoughtfully designed terraces extend the home beyond its walls, enhancing openness while preserving privacy. Integrated linear lighting emphasizes the architectural edges and adds atmosphere after sunset. The project offers a refined lifestyle defined by calm luxury and functional elegance.
